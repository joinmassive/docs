---
title: 'Accounting'
icon: 'calculator'
iconType: 'light'
description: 'If you prefer to segregate requests for your customer invoicing or internal accounting, subaccounts can be provided.'
---

| Key         | Value                                                                                                                     | Examples             |
|-------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------|
| subaccount  | Any unique identifier of up to 255 characters (regardless of character encoding); Massive will bill requests made from subaccounts separately | user@mail.com, user1 |

Example:

```bash
curl -x https://network.joinmassive.com:65535 \
     -U '{PROXY_USERNAME}-subaccount-customer@example.com:{API_KEY}' \
     https://cloudflare.com/cdn-cgi/trace
```
